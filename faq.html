What is Ingredly?

Ingredly is a smart nutrition tracking app that uses AI to scan food and ingredients, providing detailed nutritional information and health insights. Simply point your camera at any food item or ingredient label, and get instant nutritional analysis.

How does food scanning work?

Ingredly uses advanced AI and machine learning to analyze food images:

  • Point your camera at food items, meals, or ingredient labels
  • Our AI analyzes the image in real-time
  • Get instant nutritional breakdown including calories, protein, carbs, fat, and more
  • Save scans to your meal history for tracking

Which devices are supported?

Ingredly supports iPhone and iPad running iOS 13.0 or later. We recommend using newer devices with better cameras for optimal scanning accuracy.

Do I need an internet connection?

Yes, an internet connection is required for food scanning and nutritional data analysis. Some features like viewing previously saved meals may work offline.

ü•ó Nutritional Data & Accuracy

Where does nutritional data come from?

Our nutritional data comes from trusted sources:

  • USDA National Nutrient Database: Comprehensive food composition data
  • FDA Guidelines: Standard nutritional values and daily recommendations
  • Scientific Research: Studies on food additives and health impacts

Source: USDA FoodData Central

All data is provided for informational purposes only. For medical advice, consult a healthcare professional.

How accurate are the scans?

Scan accuracy depends on several factors:

  • Image quality: Clear, well-lit photos produce better results
  • Label visibility: Ensure nutrition labels are fully visible and in focus
  • Food recognition: Common foods are recognized more accurately

You can always manually edit nutritional values if needed.

Can I edit nutritional information?

Yes! After scanning, you can:

  • Tap the "Edit" button on any scan result
  • Adjust serving sizes with quick multipliers (0.5x, 1x, 1.5x, 2x)
  • Fine-tune individual nutrients with sliders
  • Reset to original values anytime

What's the health score?

For packaged products, we provide a health score (1-10) based on:

  • Nutritional quality (protein, fiber, vitamins)
  • Potentially harmful additives
  • Processing level (ultra-processed vs. whole foods)
  • Sugar, sodium, and saturated fat content

Higher scores indicate healthier products.

Is Ingredly a medical app?

No. Ingredly is designed for informational and educational purposes only. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Always consult with a qualified healthcare professional for medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment.

üì∏ Scanning Tips

How can I get better scan results?

Follow these tips for accurate scans:

  • Good lighting: Scan in bright, natural light when possible
  • Steady hands: Hold your phone steady to avoid blur
  • Clear view: Ensure the entire nutrition label or food item is visible
  • Close-up: Get close enough to capture details clearly
  • Flat surface: For labels, try to capture them head-on, not at an angle

What can I scan?

You can scan two types of items:

  • Meals: Plates of food, restaurant dishes, home-cooked meals
  • Products: Packaged foods with nutrition labels and ingredient lists

For best results with meals, ensure all food items are clearly visible.

Why did my scan fail?

Common reasons for scan failures:

  • Poor lighting or blurry image
  • Nutrition label is partially hidden or damaged
  • Food item is too complex or mixed together
  • Unstable internet connection

Try again with better conditions or manually enter the nutritional information.
